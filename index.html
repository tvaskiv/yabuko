The "Yabluko" textbooks to learn Ukrainian as a second language

Textbooks written by a team of educators according to the latest communicative methodology to learn Ukrainian as a second language

“Yabluko” is a tree-level, unique contemporary multi-level course of the Ukrainian language and culture that takes students of Ukrainian as a second language from the Elementary to Advanced level

It is the first in a series of thoroughly-structured course books in the language to be based on the most recent principles of communicative approach.

At the same time, it provides more opportunities to develop reading and writing abilities, as well as more challenging content to develop students' higher-level thinking skills
